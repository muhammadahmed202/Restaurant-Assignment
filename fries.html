fries

Golden and crispy, fries are a universally loved comfort food. Made from sliced potatoes, they are deep-fried until they achieve a delightful crunchy exterior and a soft, fluffy inside. Often seasoned with salt or various spices, fries are commonly served as a side dish or a snack. Whether paired with a juicy burger or enjoyed on their own, these potato delights are a classic and satisfying treat enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.
