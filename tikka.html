tikka

Chicken tikka is a popular Indian dish featuring marinated and grilled chicken pieces. The chicken is typically marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, which infuses it with a rich flavor. After marination, the chicken is skewered and grilled or baked until it achieves a delicious char and tenderness. Chicken tikka is often served with mint chutney and accompanied by naan or rice. This dish is beloved for its succulent texture and the aromatic blend of spices that make it a flavorful and satisfying culinary experience.
